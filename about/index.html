About

My name is Drew.

I am a card-carrying Information Scientist. Which to me means I enjoy work that involves collecting, sorting, organizing and making sense of the vast amounts of data, text and other meaningful symbols that drive societies and cultures.

In a past life I have attempted to be: an anthropologist; a novelist; a poet; a diplomat; and a hard hitting investigative journalist. In no particular order. Though none of these careers have panned out, I see them all as preparation towards working in general to make sense of the "blooming, buzzing confusion" around us.
